FAQ

What are Liddweb areas of focus?

As a full-service digital product design agency, we aim to cover the whole process of product creation, starting with analysis and strategy that lead to the design and development of a product.

We always start with defining your business needs and as a result, either work on your product from scratch or jump into a specific stage.

Take a look at the list of all services we provide or simply drop us a line — and we’ll discuss your request personally.

How do you work with AI?

For routine tasks, we use AI to optimize processes, accelerate execution, and facilitate rapid idea generation. We integrate AI functionalities into the products to enhance the user experience, elevate product quality, and add product value.

Which industries do you work with?

As a broad-spectrum digital product design agency, we work with all industries that might need a digital product, since our experience and workflow allow us to adapt to each of them. At the same time, we’ve developed the biggest expertise in Fintech, Logistics, EdTech, HRTech, and Healthcare.

Does your digital product design agency also provide product development?

Yes. As a full-scale digital product design agency, we do everything from strategy to product management, design, and development. We offer a full-scale development process, whether it’s a product, website or app.
